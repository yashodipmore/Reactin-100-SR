Store: REACTIN SR 100MG TABLET below 25°C

Marketed by: Cipla Ltd

Country of Origin: India

MRP: ₹67.65

Reactin 100 SR Tablet belongs to a group of medicines called non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs). It is used for short-term relief of pain, inflammation, and swelling in conditions that affect joints and muscles. It works by blocking chemical messengers in the brain that tell us we have pain. It can help relieve pain in conditions like rheumatoid arthritis and osteoarthritis.

How to Use Reactin Tablet PR

  • Dosage: Take Reactin Tablet PR exactly as prescribed by your healthcare provider. Do not take more than the recommended dosage.
  • To get the most benefit, take this medication regularly at the same time each day. Do not take more or use it for a longer time than needed, as it can be dangerous.
  • It is generally recommended to take the lowest effective dose for the shortest possible time.
  • Take Reactin Tablet PR with food or milk to prevent stomach upset.

Precautions

  • Reactin 100 SR Tablet should be used with precaution in patients with asthma and cardiovascular bleeding.
  • This medicine is contraindicated for the treatment of perioperative pain in the setting of coronary artery bypass graft (CABG) surgery.
  • Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult their doctors before using this medicine as it may cause harmful effects to the developing baby.

Safety Advice

Alcohol

It is unsafe to consume alcohol with Reactin 100 SR Tablet.

Pregnancy

Consult your doctor before taking Reactin 100 SR Tablet if you are pregnant.

Consult Your Doctor

Breast feeding

Reactin 100 SR Tablet is unsafe to use during pregnancy as there is definite evidence of risk to the developing baby. However, the doctor may rarely prescribe it in some life-threatening situations if the benefits are more than the potential risks. Please consult your doctor.

Driving

Unsafe

Reactin 100 SR Tablet may cause side effects which could affect your ability to drive. It may cause headaches, blurred vision, dizziness, or drowsiness in some patients. This may affect your ability to drive.

Kidney

Caution

Reactin 100 SR Tablet should be used with caution in patients with kidney disease. Dose adjustment of Reactin 100 SR Tablet may be needed. Please consult your doctor.

Do's

  • Inform your doctor if you have any history of heart disease or stroke.
  • Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult their doctors before using this medicine
  • Inform your doctor if you have liver or kidney disease, are pregnant, or breastfeeding.
  • If you miss a dose of Reactin 100 SR Tablet, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule.
  • It may cause dizziness, drowsiness or visual disturbances. Use caution while driving or doing anything that requires concentration.
  • Consult your doctor if the common side effects persist or if you’re worried about them

Do'ts

  • Do not take more or longer than needed as that can be dangerous
  • Do not double the dose in case if you miss one.
  • Do not alcohol while taking Reactin 100 SR Tablet as it can cause excessive drowsiness and increase your risk of stomach problems.
